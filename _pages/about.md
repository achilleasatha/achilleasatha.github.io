### Hi there 👋 

I'm a data scientist currently working at [Rimo3](https://rimo3.com/). Trying to solve some of the tough challenges
around software packaging, testing and deployment automation!

I'm passionate about:

- Reinforcement learning 🤖
- ML Engineering & ML Ops ☁️🚀
- Experimentation 🔬

#### What I'm working on at the moment

Currently I am working on domain-specific applications of **computer vision** for object detection & recognition and building
**reinforcement learning** agents that can intelligently interact with the contextual environment to automate repetitive human 
tasks in IT.

#### Profession Experience

I have professional experience in experimentation (**Frequentist** & **Bayesian**), both for multivariate (A/B/n) testing and 
**Multi-Armed Bandit** experiments, with a strong focus on testing of ML solutions to understand and measure 
user-facing impact. 

I've been involved in developing and productionizing a large-scale experimentation platform on
the cloud, along with a major tech-stack migration from on-premise to the cloud.

#### Volunteering
